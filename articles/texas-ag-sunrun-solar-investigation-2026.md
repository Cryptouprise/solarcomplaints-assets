### Does the Texas AG investigation automatically cancel my Sunrun or Freedom Forever contract?

No. You do not get an automatic exit just because the state is investigating. The AG can seek injunctions, restitution, and penalties against the company, but your individual contract still stands until you take action on your own case. That is why moving now matters — the evidence the AG is forcing these companies to hand over becomes ammunition for your individual claim.

### I signed my solar contract three years ago. Is it too late to do anything?

Probably not. Texas DTPA claims generally run two years from the date you discovered the deception — not from when you signed the contract. If you just realized the savings were misrepresented, your clock may have started this week. Get a case review before you assume you are out of options.

### Freedom Forever filed Chapter 11 bankruptcy. Does that kill my claim?

No. A Chapter 11 reorganization does not erase homeowner claims — it changes how they are handled. You may need to file a proof of claim in the bankruptcy court to preserve your rights. And your solar loan is a separate contract with a separate lender, which means it survives the installer's bankruptcy entirely.
